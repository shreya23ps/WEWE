About ABHA: Empowering Rural Transformation

Founded by Rahul Amrutkar in 2020, ABHA is an ambitious initiative to spark rural entrepreneurship and drive economic transformation under the AatmaNirbhar Bharat mission. Our vision is to turn villages into self-sustaining hubs of innovation and prosperity.
ABHA invites architects, engineers,entrepreneurs, investors, and government bodies to join our mission to transform rural India. Together, we can create a more sustainable, prosperous, and inclusive future.

  • Vision
  • Initiatives
  • Benefits

ABHA's Vision

  • Over Competition: We focus on unity and shared success.
  • Sustainable Livelihoods: Empowering rural communities through self-employment and entrepreneurship.
  • Rural Transformation: Creating thriving, balanced, and prosperous villages across India

Key ABHA Initiatives

  • IITe (Indian Industrialists and Technocrats Estates): Developing small-scale, sustainable industrial estates in rural areas to empower MSMEs and decentralize industrial growth
  • Waste Treatment Innovations: Partnering with Patpert Technologies to turn waste plastics into valuable pyrolysis oil, fostering sustainable waste management solutions.
  • Village Balance Sheets: A framework for monitoring and strategizing rural transformation, ensuring balanced, transparent growth.
  • Gaukendrit Gymkhana: A reverse migration model that combines residential and industrial development over 100+ acres for a balanced, happy lifestyle.
  • Atal Gotha Factory: A circular economy model that integrates village resources with technology to foster rural development.

The Benefits of ABHA's Rural Transformation

  • Decentralized Growth: Reducing reliance on urban centers and fostering resilient rural economies.
  • Social Impact: Promoting community well-being, bridging gaps, and addressing inequalities.
  • Empowerment: Enabling rural communities to thrive through entrepreneurship and self-reliance.
